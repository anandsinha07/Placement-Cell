ACTIVITIES : TRAINING

  1. To arrange workshops and seminars on technical, aptitude, interview-handling skills, interpersonal skills, time management, stress management and failure management.
  2. To arrange lectures by corporate experts.
  3. To arrange special training program on soft skill and aptitude for campus recruitment process.

ACTIVITIES : PLACEMENT

  1. To invite corporate companies for on campus recruitment process of final year students.
  2. To schedule campus visit of companies.
  3. To coordinate the recruitment schedule of the day.
  4. To coordinate post selection process like distribution of offers letters and keeping them updated about the latest companies.
  5. To work as a catalyst between selected student and the respected company.
  6. To receive feedback from HR of the company.
