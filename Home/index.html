The Training and Placement cell is headed by Dr. S. S. Narkhede (TPO) who is keen in encouraging students to prefer job that better suits their profile. The placement season commences in the first month of every academic year and continues till the end of academic year. Pre-Placement talks are conducted for the final year students prior to the placement session every year.

The T&P cell has adequate infrastructure to accomodate all teh activities related to training and placement of PICT students. It has comapny operation rooms, interview rooms and student coordination rooms. The T&P cell is assisted by a committee comprising of students representative from UG and PG classes.
